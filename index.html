Save the forests

Deforestation has become a crisis for us all. Find out what you can do to fight to save the forests!

Deforestation is the action of clearing forests. It has truly harmful effects and has become a threat to the amazonian people and the habitats of animals. It has become such an important issue that we must minimise to reduce global warming!



Myths about deforestation

Although deforestation is an important issue today, many of us are still blinded by misconceptions.


Below are 3 key misconceptions:


Myth 1: We can only solve this if we all go vegan

Although going vegan is a great lifestyle change and can help this issue, it is more important to make much better use of our lands while continuing to eat meat and dairy


Myth 2: Most trees are cut down for paper or wood

Although huge demands for wood and timber are an unsurprising threat, causes like the demand for meat, soya beans, and palm oil are in fact the major causes


Myth 3: Deforestation is inevitable

No! The most tragic thing about deforestation is that people believe that it's inevtiable and there's no point stopping it. By reducing waste and making lifestyle changes, land management and deforestation practices can becomes a lot of sustainable!

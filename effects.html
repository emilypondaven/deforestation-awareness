Why are we fighting deforestation?

Forests are vital for the health of our planet. They allow as to breath, and provide food and shelter for both animals and humans on earth.


Deforestation has huge effects on both the communities surrounding the area as well as animals living in the forest. These harmful impacts are felt particularly today in the Amazon rainforest. Below are some major effects:



Environmental effects

  • Climate change due to increased greenhouse gases
  • Habitat loss, causing biodiversity losses
  • Soil erosion and flooding
  • Water cycle disruptions which can have big influences on rainfall patterns as well as water and soil quality.


Social effects

  • Destruction of homelands, particularly for indigenous communities who depend on the forest to sustain their way of life
